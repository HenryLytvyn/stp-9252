How to Play

Simple Moves. Deep Thinking.

  • 1

    Tap a tile to move it to your holding tray

  • 2

    Match 3 of the same design to remove them

  • 3

    Don't overfill the tray - that ends your round

  • 4

    Use shuffle or undo when necessary

  • 5

    Earn coins and stars to unlock themes and levels

  • 6

    New tile sets are introduced as you progress

From the first tap, you'll feel the satisfying flow of clean, purposeful motion.
